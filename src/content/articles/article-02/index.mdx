**Horseshoe Crab Monitoring at Tiana Bayside**  
_Monday, June 23, 2025 – 8:05 PM to 10:05 PM (Doors at 7:55 PM)_

**UNDER 14 WITH PARENT OR LEGAL GUARDIAN**  
_Part of the Tiana Horseshoe Crab Monitoring collection_

Horseshoe Crabs have been around for over 450 million years! Any vaccine or medical implant must be tested with a compound found in their blood to detect bacteria. Join us in a state‑wide effort to monitor their population and inform policy that will help sustain them for generations to come.

Back to the Bays, an initiative of CCE Marine Program, oversees the monitoring at the Tiana Bayside site, coordinated by Daniell Morris (dm733@cornell.edu).

- **Location:** Tiana Bayside Facility  
  89 Dune Road, Hampton Bays, NY 11946
- **Free venue parking**
- **Refund Policy:** No Refunds

**How to prepare:**

- Dress for the weather, rain or shine (only thunderstorms may cause cancellations)
- Wear waders, tall boots, or water shoes when water is warm enough
- Bring extra flashlights and headlamps
- Instructions, data sheets, clipboards, and tools are provided
- Familiarize yourself with the Sample Data Sheet (waterproof sheets will be provided)
- Review Quadrat + Count Protocols (volunteer coordinator will teach methods)
